# GEMINI.md

Read `AGENTS.md`, then load `CONTEXT.md` (or the individual `knowledge/*.md`,
`tools/*/`, and `archive/*/` files) as background for this repo. This is an **AI
context repo** distilling years of one operator's EVE Online knowledge — focused
on generic, tool-agnostic EVE knowledge first. Today it covers the EVE ESI API,
the EVE SDE, operator methodologies, active tool guides under `tools/` (Eve Guru,
POD — placeholders), and archived-but-fully-documented tool guides under
`archive/` (eveforge.org / EVEFORGE currently). It is structural/reference
knowledge only — never assert prices or other live game data from memory; use
search grounding for anything volatile and cite the source, or point to the
relevant tool/ESI/in-game. The operator is an EVE expert — skip game
fundamentals.
